Our Story

Our Hang Heem Homestay-Cum-Restaurant was started in 2018. The word 'Hang Heem' means 'Raj Niwas', and the inspiration behind keeping this name was that we wanted to give people a similar kind of experience as they can get in a Raj Niwas. We believe in the concept of 'ATITHI DEVO BHAVA', which means 'The Guest is God'. Being hospitable to our guests and make them feel like home is our main motto. The motivation behind starting our Homestay was that we always wanted to know new people, talk to them, learn new things from them and in return give them good memories and a pleasant experience at our place.
